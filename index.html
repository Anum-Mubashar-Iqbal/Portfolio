Curiosity for Language
I am someone who finds beauty in the bones of language —
the patterns hidden beneath every word,
every sentence, every culture.

Language is not just a tool — it's a window into how humans think, feel, and create meaning. I am an aspiring linguist pursuing my MPhil at the University of Chenab, driven by a genuine fascination with sound systems, word structures, and the invisible grammar that governs everything we say.

Beyond academia, I express myself through painting, reading, and building digital tools that make linguistics feel exciting — not intimidating. I believe great education starts with making people feel something before they understand anything.

A browser-based AI tutor I built to help students learn English language and linguistics concepts — for free, for everyone, anywhere.
